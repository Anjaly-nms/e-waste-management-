Our Aims

What is our school's aim?


The major objective of our e-waste management is to reduce, reuse, and recycle. Some of the e-waste consists of valuable covering or materials inside which can be reused or recycled. Whereas some of the e-waste may contain hazardous chemical materials which should be disposed of carefully without causing harm to nature




Our mission is to encourage recycling by regularly introducing latest e-waste technologies and on the other side protect natural resources and eliminate landfills by utilizing innovative technology.

Facts

Lets see some companies, and what they do regarding the problemas of E-waste

Apple:

Apple has implemented a comprehensive recycling program, offering customers the option to return old Apple products for recycling or trade-in when purchasing new ones. Through its "Apple GiveBack" program, the company aims to reduce electronic waste and recover valuable materials from old devices.

Dell:

Dell operates a global recycling program called "Dell Reconnect," which allows consumers to drop off old electronics at participating locations for responsible recycling. The company also incorporates recycled materials into its products and packaging to reduce its environmental footprint.


Samsung:

Samsung Electronics has established e-waste recycling programs in many countries, providing convenient options for customers to recycle their old electronics. The company also focuses on designing products with recyclability in mind and uses recycled materials in its manufacturing processes.
